How Aegis
works for you.

01

You ask a question

Describe the decision you're facing in plain language. No special formatting required — Aegis understands context naturally.

Every relevant factor is weighed and compared. Trade-offs are identified, alternatives are considered, and reasoning is built step-by-step.
